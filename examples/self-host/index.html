lcc-wasm — a self-hosted C compiler, running in your browser

The LCC C compiler itself (front end + our WebAssembly back end) compiled to rcc.wasm — it compiles the C below straight to a binary .wasm module that the browser runs directly. No server, no native toolchain, no assembler — just the ~280 KB compiler. Based on LCC by C. W. Fraser & D. R. Hanson — free for research/instructional use; see CPYRIGHT.
